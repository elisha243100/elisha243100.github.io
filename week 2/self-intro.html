My name is Ong Elisha Bei Wei. I'm 19 years old this year and i'm from Ipoh, Perak. I currently a student studying at Dasein Academy of Arts taking Diploma in Digital Media. In my free time, I enjoy doomscrolling on my phone, playing games with friends or family, and watching tv series or action movies. I also enjoy drawing, but I don't do it as often as I would like to. My favorite food nowadays is either curry noodles and shredded chicken noodles, and I love to drink milk teas, especially Chagee's Tie Guan Yin Milk Tea. I chose this course of studies is because i'm very interested in arts and design, but as the world keeps evolving, I want to be at the forefront of this change, so instead of taking the traditional drawing route, I wanted to challenge myself and learn digital media. In the future, I hope to work in a creative industry that combines technology and creativity to make a positive impact on people's lives.
